Business Process Automation

What is business process automation?

Business process automation (BPA) uses software to perform repetitive, rule-based tasks automatically — such as data entry, invoice processing, report generation, document routing, and system integrations. Automating these tasks reduces human error, saves staff time, and speeds up operations significantly.

What is the difference between RPA and business process automation?

RPA (Robotic Process Automation) uses software robots to mimic human interactions with existing applications — clicking, copying, form filling. BPA is broader and includes workflow engines, system integrations, and custom automation tools. MazeQube delivers both, selecting the best approach for each process.

Which processes are best suited to automation?

The best candidates are high-volume, repetitive, rule-based tasks with low exception rates — such as invoice processing, purchase order creation, employee onboarding, data migration between systems, scheduled reporting, and customer notification workflows.

How long does it take to automate a business process?

Simple automation projects can be delivered in 2–4 weeks. Complex end-to-end process automation with multiple system integrations typically takes 6–12 weeks. We recommend starting with a pilot automation to demonstrate ROI before scaling.

AI Solutions & AI Agents

What is an AI agent?

An AI agent is a software system that can autonomously perform multi-step business tasks — unlike a simple chatbot that answers scripted questions. AI agents can reason about goals, use tools, access live data, and take actions across multiple systems to complete complex tasks with minimal human involvement.

How are AI agents different from chatbots?

Chatbots answer questions from a fixed script or knowledge base. AI agents can reason, plan, use tools, access live data, and take actions across multiple systems. An AI agent does not just tell you the answer — it can go and perform the task itself.

Can MazeQube integrate AI into our existing software?

Yes. MazeQube specialises in integrating AI capabilities — including large language models, document intelligence, and predictive analytics — into existing business applications. We work with your current systems rather than requiring replacement.

Do we need large amounts of data to implement AI?

Not always. Modern pre-trained AI models can deliver value with little to no custom training data. For document classification, email triage, and Q&A systems, we integrate existing AI models that work out of the box. Custom model training is only required for highly specialised prediction tasks.

Is AI integration GDPR compliant?

Yes. MazeQube designs all AI integrations to be GDPR compliant by default — using privacy-preserving architectures, data minimisation, audit trails, and where necessary on-premise or private cloud deployment to ensure data stays within your control.

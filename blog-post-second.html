Business Networking

Our client from New York, who manages a business community by hosting events and meetups, initially contracted a company from Russia to build their application using WordPress. Unfortunately, the delivered solution did not meet their expectations. After understanding their dissatisfaction, it became clear that the client needed a CMS that was both easy to maintain and user-friendly.

Given that this was the second attempt to fulfill their needs, and recognizing the client’s previous negative experience, we offered a cost-effective solution. We presented a new design and took on the challenge of developing a fresh application rather than modifying the existing, inadequate code. Our approach included migrating all data from the old application to the new one and incorporating additional plugins to address specific image processing requirements that the client was unable to manage.

Our commitment to staying ahead in delivery and adopting a customer-first approach led to a successful project outcome. By simplifying the application and providing ongoing support, we not only helped our client stay competitive but also demonstrated our belief that business success is built on effective partnerships and innovative solutions.
